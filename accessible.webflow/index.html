FAQ & support

Accessibility made simple

Get answers to your top accessibility and compliance questions.

What does accessibility compliance mean?

Accessibility compliance ensures your website is usable for everyone, including people with disabilities, by following standards like WCAG 2.1. This helps meet legal obligations and improves user experience.

Which website platforms are supported?

We support Shopify, Squarespace, Magento, WordPress, Wix, Webflow, and other leading platforms. Solutions are tailored to your site’s requirements.

How do I select a plan?

Choose a plan based on your site’s size, features, and compliance needs. Options range from basic audits to full remediation and ongoing support.

What’s included in your service?

Services include a comprehensive audit, compliance report, recommended fixes, and implementation support. Ongoing monitoring is available to maintain standards.
